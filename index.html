Empowering Silent Voices Through Technology.

SmartSpecs seeks to normalise sign language and empower people who are mute and/or deaf to communicate effortlessly with those close to them. These innovative glasses are equipped with a camera that reads sign language and translates it into spoken words or text, fostering inclusivity and enhancing social integration.

Features

  • Gesture recognition

    Accurately detects and interprets sign language gestures

  • Real-Time Translation

    Instantly translates sign language into audible words or text displayed on our companion app.

  • User-Friendly Interface

    Intuitive and easy-to-use interface for seamless operation, making it accessible for all age groups and tech skill levels.

Featured App:

The companion app for SmartSpecs not only displays translated text but also showcases the corresponding sign language gestures. This dual display helps people understand the meaning behind the signs and offers an opportunity to learn sign language while communicating, promoting better understanding and smoother interactions.

Earpiece Communication:

When both individuals wear SmartSpecs, they can enhance their communication experience through detachable earpieces. These earpieces transmit the translated spoken words directly to the listener, ensuring clear and immediate auditory feedback. Additionally, SmartSpecs can replace hearing aids, providing a streamlined and integrated solution for those with hearing impairments, further enhancing accessibility and communication efficiency.
